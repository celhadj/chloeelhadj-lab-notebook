What I'm exploring

I am interested in organizations built on expertise, trust, and long-term relationships. As AI becomes part of these industries, the challenge is not simply adopting new technology, but rather it is understanding where it genuinely improves the way people work while preserving the human judgment that makes those organizations valuable.
