The lane

I am interested in organizations built on expertise, trust, and long-term relationships. These businesses are not looking for AI to replace people, they are looking for better ways to support the work people already do. This notebook explores where AI creates genuine operational value, where human judgment remains essential, and thoughtful implementation can strengthen rather than disrupt these organizations.
