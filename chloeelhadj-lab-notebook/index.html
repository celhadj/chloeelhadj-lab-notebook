Harney & Sons — Wholesale Order Routing

A 150-year-old tea company, one manual routing step, and a prototype built to test whether it's actually worth automating. →

How each entry works

01Sit inside the workflow until I can draw it exactly, not approximately.
02Find the one step that's repetitive, not judgment — and name the step that isn't.
03Build the prototype. Not a slide about it — the thing itself.
04Publish what happened, including the parts that didn't work.

The lane

Fine art, wealth advising, specialty goods — none of them sell a product. They sell discretion. Automate the wrong step and you erase the thing the client was paying for. Find the right one and the expert gets more time to be an expert. That's the specific problem this lab exists to get good at.
