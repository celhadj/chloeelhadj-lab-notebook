How AI fits into organizations.

Each entry here begins with a real operational problem. I start by understanding how the organization works, then I identify where AI can create value, build and test a prototype, and document what I learn along the way. The goal isn't just to build the software, but also to better understand how AI fits into organizations.

Projects are added as they are completed. The emphasis is on documenting real work rather than publishing on a schedule.
