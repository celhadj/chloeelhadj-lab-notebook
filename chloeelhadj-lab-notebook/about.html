About me: Chloe El Hadj

I study Political Science and Art History at Colgate University. This website is a place for me to document projects exploring how AI can improve the way businesses operate.

How I got interested

During the summer of 2026, I interned at Harney & Sons Teas, where I worked with accounting and production. What began as a traditional business internship quickly became an opportunity for me to find workflows that could be redesigned with AI. I mapped business processes and built data pipelines using live data. Along the way, I realized that implementing AI is not just a technical challenge, but also an organizational one.

What I'm interested in

Rather than asking what AI can automate, I am interested in finding where it can create meaningful leverage. Current areas of interest include workflow design, business operations, and the practical challenges of introducting new technology into established organizations.

Why this website

This website is a place to document projects, ideas, and experiments. My hope is that, over time, these notes will show not only what I have built, but how my understanding of AI implementation continues to evolve.
