I study at Colgate University, intending to major in Art History and Political Science. I made this website as a place for me to document projects exploring how AI can improve the way businesses operate.

How I got interested

During a summer internship at a 150-year-old family company, I walked in expecting to spend the summer 100% in accounting and bookkeeping. While this was definitely a part of my internship, I was also exposed to thinking about the business processes behind the work. I mapped workflows and experimented with AI tools, realizing that implementing AI is not just a technical challenge, but also an organizational one.

What I'm interested in

I am interested in finding areas where AI can create meaningful value in workflow design, business operations, and the challenges of introducing new technology into established organizations.

Why this website

This website is for me to document projects, ideas, and experiments. My hope is that, over time, these notes will show not only what I have built, but also how my understanding of AI continues to evolve.
