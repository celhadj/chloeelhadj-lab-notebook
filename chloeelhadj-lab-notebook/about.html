I study Political Science and Art History at Colgate University. This website is a place for me to document projects exploring how AI can improve the way businesses operate.

How I got interested

During a summer internship at a 150-year-old family company, I expected to spend the summer learning about accounting. While this was definitely a part of my internship, I also became interested in the business processes behind the work. I mapped business processes and experimented with AI tools, realizing that implementing AI is not just a technical challenge, but also an organizational one.

What I'm interested in

I am interested in finding where AI can create meaningful value in workflow design, business operations, and the practical challenges of introducing new technology into established organizations.

Why this website

This website is for me to document projects, ideas, and experiments. My hope is that, over time, these notes will show not only what I have built, but also how my understanding of AI implementation continues to evolve.
