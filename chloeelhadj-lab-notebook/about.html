About

I am a sophomore at Colgate University, majoring in Political Science and Art History. Recently, I am interested in AI implementation.

I enjoy understanding an organization's workflow, figuring out its operations, and asking if AI can remove repetitive work without replacing the human judgment that makes the business special.

Where this started

During my internship at Harney & Sons Fine Teas, I became interested in the workflow behind the business. I experimented with AI tools like Claude and Structify to see whether repetitive parts of the company's processes could be simplified.

What I actually do

Right now, I'm learning by building and mapping workflows, identifying repetitive tasks, and creating prototypes of ideas using tools like Claude and Structify. I'm interested in AI implementation more than AI engineering; I am trying to understand businesses well enough to recognize where AI can help and where it will not.
